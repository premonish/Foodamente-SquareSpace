How I Lost 23 Pounds in 6 Months

Foodamente
November 14, 2017

“Losing weight is easy!”

How I wish this was true way back. It wasn’t. But then I discovered the best and most effective diet for me. This could work for you too!

So I’d like to share my weight loss story.

In high school, I was very active, playing so many sports - softball, table tennis, badminton, volleyball, soccer. Moving to college, I was so crazy about soccer that I became our football club’s Team Captain. In law school, I kept active playing soccer.

I knew I was always around 120 pounds. Then time, activities, stress level, partying, binging added up to it and by early 2017, I was 141 pounds.

I was shocked and thought to myself,

“Oh my gosh, I need to lose weight!”

All the while I thought I was okay since I was doing a lot more physical activities, including new hobbies such as boxing, rock climbing, futsal, running  half-marathons and all that.

I carried with me the wrong notion that I can equalize my not-so-healthy diet by being physically active.

I stopped eating rice as early as 2015 and only have rice occasionally. But then I replaced rice with a lot of bread. I ate a lot of noodles like ramen. I carbo-load during pre-runs. I was socially drinking my favorite margarita. I was eating some salads with carb-rich dressings.

The Challenge

Around March 2017, I was challenged to lose 5 pounds by my partner. The first person to lose 5 pounds would earn a mini-vacation paid for by the other person.

With the realization that I gained so much weight coupled with the challenge, I got serious and was determined to strategize on what diet could work.

I am a legal researcher by profession so I began to dig around for solutions. The diet that consistently pops up in my research is - the LOW-CARB DIET.

LOW CARBOHYDRATE DIET BENEFITS - Harvard School of Public Health

I was wrong with eliminating just rice but still eating a lot of carbohydrate-rich food.

  • bread
  • pasta
  • noodles
  • salad with carb-rich dressing
  • margaritas

I had bad eating habits.

“The only way to have effective weight loss results is to change my eating habits,”

I said. And so I did.

I became a convert.

A convert from high-carb diet to a LOW-CARB DIET. The best thing about the diet is I can eat whatever I want to eat during Cheat Days. It helps in the weight loss process too.

Winner Declared
My partner won the challenge by converting to a LOW-CARB DIET as well. I was encouraged to continue as I lost 4 pounds in one week. We went on to challenge each other again and again and after 6 months of eating LOW-CARB, I had lost 23 pounds - 141 lbs. - 118 lbs!

WEIGHT LOSS ACHIEVED

"I succeeded in going from 141 pounds in early 2017 down to 118 pounds in September 2017. My weight-loss partner also lost more than 20 pounds in the same period. It is so amazing that I am so excited to share the diet with everyone."

Come join me and lose weight now!

Attorney Pinky Veloso
